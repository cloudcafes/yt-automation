SCENE 1 — Sky Above the Kingdom / Tower Exterior — Curiosity ? Wonder
START EMOTION: Gentle curiosity about the world and the tall tower
END EMOTION: Soft wonder at the magical tower and the girl inside
TRIGGER: Seeing how high the tower reaches and hearing there is a girl with a magical secret at the top

Shot 1: Wide aerial shot (long shot) of a sunny kingdom that looks like a giant playground below, with soft 3D houses and tiny people; the camera slowly tilts up to a single, very tall tower reaching toward the clouds, showing the “tickling the clouds” feeling. Light sparkles on windows, wind makes little flags flap, hinting at magic.
Shot 2: Medium shot from a low angle at the tower base, looking up the endless stone walls as the wind whooshes around; the sunlight wraps the tower in a warm glow, making it feel safe and magical, not scary.
Shot 3: Closeup on the very top window of the tower, the glass catching the sun like a tiny star; inside we only see a soft silhouette of a girl with long hair, teasing her “magical secret” and filling kids with wonder.

VISUAL CONTINUITY NOTES:
Character appearance: Only a soft silhouette of Radhita is seen, with very long hair flowing past her shoulders. No clear face yet, just the idea of her.
Symbolic motif: Light vs dark (bright sky and shining tower against soft blue distance); exploration (camera traveling upward).
Color palette: Warm golds and creamy whites for the tower, bright sky-blue, soft pastel greens for the kingdom below.

START EMOTION: Quiet loneliness in a small but cozy world
END EMOTION: Curious longing for the world outside
TRIGGER: Radhita looking out of the window and wondering what is out there

Shot 2: Closeup of Radhita’s face as she sings softly; her eyes close and relax, and her hair glows gently like morning sunlight, lighting the room with a magical golden shine. Pascal sits on her shoulder, copying her expressions, and Maximus is visible resting near the door.
Shot 3: Over-the-shoulder shot from behind Radhita as she presses her hands on the window, looking out at the bright outside world; inside is warm and dimmer, outside is bright and open, showing her longing and new curiosity.

VISUAL CONTINUITY NOTES:
Character appearance: Radhita has very long, glowing golden hair, simple soft dress (pastel purple or cream), bare feet; Pascal is a tiny green chameleon, Maximus is a strong white or light-colored horse. All look clean and well-kept.
Symbolic motif: Freedom vs confinement (cozy room vs open window); growth (her curiosity stretching beyond walls).
Color palette: Warm yellows and oranges inside; cool bright blues and greens outside the window to contrast.

SCENE 3 — Tower Top Room (Interior, Later with Mother Gothel) — Tension ? Relief
START EMOTION: Gentle curiosity and quiet questioning about the outside
END EMOTION: Forced relief / fake safety under Mother Gothel’s rules
TRIGGER: Mother Gothel’s dramatic entrance and scary warnings about the outside world

Shot 2: Medium two-shot of Gothel bending down toward Radhita, stroking her hair while speaking; Radhita looks up with mixed feelings—she tries to smile but her eyes show a tiny worry. The lighting stays warm but a bit dimmer around Gothel, giving her a shadowy edge.
Shot 3: Closeup of Radhita after Gothel turns away; she forces a small smile and hugs her hair like a blanket, trying to feel safe again. The camera gently pulls back to show the window behind her, bright but far away, hinting that the relief is not truly happy.

Character appearance: Radhita’s long glowing hair and dress unchanged; Gothel always in dark, rich colors with a big cloak, neat hair, and slightly sharp features.
Symbolic motif: Freedom vs confinement (Gothel’s body blocking the window); light vs dark (warm light on Radhita, shadowy edges on Gothel).
Color palette: Warm golds and browns in the room; Gothel’s reds and blacks add contrast and slight tension.
