SCENE 1 The Tower Room Loneliness → Curiosity
START EMOTION: Loneliness
END EMOTION: Curiosity
TRIGGER: Seeing the floating lights appear in the distance.
Shot 1: Medium shot Radhita sighs, her shoulders slumped as she stares out the window. Her golden hair lies still around her, looking more like chains than sunshine. Symbolism: The window frame acts like bars on a cage.
Shot 2: Close-up on her eyes Her eyes widen, reflecting tiny, faraway specks of light. A tiny spark of interest replaces her sad look.
Shot 3: Low angle shot She leans forward on the windowsill, her face now full of wonder and questions as she watches the lights float up. Symbolism: The low angle makes her seem small against the big, exciting world outside.
VISUAL CONTINUITY NOTES:
Character appearance: Radhita in her simple purple dress. Hair is long, golden, and neatly brushed.
Symbolic motif: Confinement the tower vs. Freedom the distant lights.
Color palette: Dim, warm interior tones vs. the cool, deep blue and sparkling gold of the night sky.

SCENE 2 The Tower Room Fear → Courage
START EMOTION: Fear
END EMOTION: Courage
TRIGGER: Flynn Rider climbing into her tower.
Shot 1: Extreme close-up Radhita's frightened eyes peer from behind a curtain. She holds a frying pan tightly, her knuckles white. Camera: Shaky, from her perspective.

Shot 3: Low angle on Radhita She stands over the sleeping Flynn, still holding the pan like a shield, but her expression is now determined and brave, not just scared.
VISUAL CONTINUITY NOTES:
Character appearance: Flynn is now present, unconscious on the floor. Radhita's hair is slightly messy from hiding.
Symbolic motif: Using an everyday object frying pan as a tool of courage.
Color palette: Still dim, but a sliver of moonlight cuts across the scene, highlighting the tense moment.

Character appearance: Her hair is a little messy from dancing. She still has her frying pan.

Character appearance: Radhita's hair is short and brown. Eugene has a small, healed scar.

Color palette: Starts with cold, dark shadows, ending in a soft, warm, magical glow.
